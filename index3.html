How To

Starter:

It's as simple as finding a bolwing ally near you and just going to play. Each bolwing ally will have it's own rules but the main thing is to just rent a lane for an hour or even by game, then find a ball that you like that fits your hand, put on some shoes and then just throw the ball at the pins.

Gear And Equipment:

Later down the road if you feel bolwing is your sport then you can get your own equipment like shoes and bowling balls. There is big benift from getting your own stuff like building the consistency with the same ball and shoes. Bowling becomes a huge game of consistency and with your own equipment you will get better in no time at.

Now there are some drawbacks to owning your own equipment like the price you have to pay to first get which could steep for some people but the bext invesment is shoes because you won't be paying the rental fee when you go bolwing. You will also have to take care of your own equipment to make sure it doesn't get ruined or even break.

Next Steps:

If you are serious about bowling the next steps you should take should be trying to join a league to maybe get a little more competive or you just want to make some new friends with the same hobby as you.
