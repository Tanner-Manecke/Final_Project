Leagues

Leagues:

A bowling league is a group of bowlers who compete regularly as teams over a set period, usually weekly or biweekly. Leagues can be casual or competitive and are often organized by skill level, age group, or even themes (like mixed-gender or senior leagues). Most leagues have a season format, where teams earn points based on match results, leading to playoffs or a championship. Participants often enjoy both the competitive side and the social atmosphere, making leagues a fun way to improve skills and meet new people. League play typically includes set rules, handicaps for balancing skill levels, and sometimes prizes or trophies.
