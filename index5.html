Inclusivity:

Bowling is a highly inclusive sport that welcomes people of all ages, skill levels, and physical abilities. Many bowling centers offer adaptive equipment—like ramps and bumpers—for those with disabilities, and there are leagues and events tailored for kids, seniors, and individuals with special needs. Its social, low-impact nature makes it accessible and enjoyable for a wide range of participants, promoting a strong sense of community and belonging.
